Tiger

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family and are renowned for their power and strength.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 3.3m
  • Average Lifespan: 20-26 years
  • Habitat: Forests and Mangroove

Tigers have muscular bodies with powerful forelimbs, large heads and long tails. The pelage is dense and heavy; colouration varies between shades of orange and brown with white ventral areas and distinctive vertical black stripes.
